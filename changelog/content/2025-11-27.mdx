**Professional Timing Control for Every Stage**  
From conferences to theaters, broadcast studios to classrooms. DYTOR delivers
drift-free synchronization and role-based control that keeps productions running
flawlessly and adapts to every team.

## Highlights

- **Dual Timer System** with count-down / count-up, pause/resume, and smart alerts.
- **Event Scheduling** with drag-and-drop queue, presets, and visual progression.
- **Message Broadcasting** with presets, flash effects, and visibility control.
- **Remote Control** with multi-device access and real-time monitoring.
- **Multi-Display Support** for primary control and secondary stage screens.
- **Analytics & Recording** with session logs and export-ready reporting.

## Role-Based Interfaces

- **Admin** - full control of timers, schedule, messages, displays, and permissions.
- **Queue Manager** - event flow coordination and preset messaging.
- **Speaker** - personal timer control and session visibility.
- **Viewer** - display-only access for audience screens and monitoring.
